There are plenty of books to read and plenty of discuss. Meet every night from 19:00 to 21:30 at the Community Center for tea and share what you read. The village Community Center is on Wood Lane.

  • Treasure Island by Robert Louis Stevenson


  • Far From The Madding Crowd by Thomas Hardy


  • James And The Giant Peach by Roald Dahl
